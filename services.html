Our Services

Rheumatologist

A rheumatologist specializes in diagnosing and treating disorders related to joints, muscles, and bones, often involving autoimmune diseases.

Physiotherapist

A physiotherapist specializes in physical conditions affecting movement, using treatments like exercises and manual therapy.

Dermatologist

A dermatologist specializes in treating conditions of the skin, hair, and nails, using medications, procedures, or surgery.

Neurologist

A neurologist diagnoses and treats conditions affecting the brain, spinal cord, and nerves, such as epilepsy and Parkinson's disease.

Orthopedic

Orthopedics focuses on diagnosing and treating musculoskeletal injuries and diseases, including bones and joints.

Physiotherapist

A physiotherapist, or physical therapist, works with patients to help them manage pain, balance, mobility, and motor function.
